Speech Emotion Recognition (SER)

Wav2Vec2, Transformers, CNN, PyTorch

Conducted a comparative study of ML and DL architectures for emotion classification on the RAVDESS dataset. Benchmarked approaches ranging from Random Forest and Spectrogram-based CNNs to Transformer models. Achieved state-of-the-art accuracy of 97.2% using a fine-tuned Wav2Vec2 model, aiming to enhance accessibility tools for the hearing and visually impaired.
